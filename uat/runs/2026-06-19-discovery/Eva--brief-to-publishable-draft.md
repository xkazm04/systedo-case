# Journal — Eva, "brief → publishable draft"

_First-person, in-character. Public `/ai-asistent`, "Obsahový brief" tool. Real Claude-CLI model calls under a 60s client ceiling._

**Why I'm here:** I've got a topic for the brand and I want the tool to take me from topic → a credible SEO brief → a draft I could actually publish, and show me how it'll look in search first.

**Open the brief tool, fill the sample, hit "Vytvořit brief".** A timer counts up… and at 60 seconds it gives up: "Model neodpověděl do 60 sekund. Zkusit znovu." Nothing to show for the wait. If this is my first impression of the tool, I'd assume it's broken. (→ EV1)

**Try again.** This time it lands at ~48 seconds — just under the wire. And it's *good*. The search preview shows exactly how it'll look on Google: `mionelo.cz › blog › skladovani-orechu-a-seminek`, "Skladování ořechů: jak je uchovat čerstvé déle", a 129-char meta. SEO score 75/100, and the scorecard is honest — it even tells me the keyword is missing from the meta and the intro, which I'd fix. The outline is seven sections that are genuinely about *our* niche — žluknutí/oxidation, the right containers, freezing, shelf-life by nut type — not interchangeable filler. FAQ, keyword set, internal-link ideas all on-brand. This is exactly the skeleton I wanted. *If it would just load reliably.*

**Now the part I actually came for — "Rozepsat článek".** This is the promise: turn the brief into a publishable draft. I click it… timer climbs… 60 seconds… timeout again. The draft never comes. So I have a lovely brief and no article — the loop I was sold doesn't close. (→ EV1)

**Did I get my job done?** Half. I can export the brief as .md and hand it to a writer — that's real value. But "a draft I could publish" — the thing that closes the loop — I never reached it; it times out every time. **Status: Blocked at the draft step** (brief succeeds, flakily; the publishable draft does not).
