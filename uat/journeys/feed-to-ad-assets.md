## Trigger (why now)
New products landed in the feed and need ads live by end of day. Klára wants asset groups built within Google's limits, an importable CSV, keyword ideas, and her negative lists updated — without the usual character-counting and upload-failure dance.

## Definition of done (her POV)
- RSA/PMax assets within exact limits (headlines ≤30, descriptions ≤90) with live counts; switching SKU doesn't leak the prior product's copy.
- A Google Ads Editor CSV that imports cleanly (delimiter/encoding correct, diacritics intact).
- Keyword ideas with intent + volume/competition, and a usable negatives workflow (tag/aggregate/export).
- Stock-aware budget guidance so she doesn't push spend at a soon-to-stock-out SKU.

## Out of scope
