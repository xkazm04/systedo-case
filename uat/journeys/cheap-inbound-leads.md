## Trigger (why now)
Radek's pipeline is thin this month. He has no budget to buy leads, so he opens the app to (a) get found organically for his service+locality and (b) make sure he answers the few enquiries he has before they go cold.

## Definition of done (their POV)
- **Keyword / local** modules tell him which service×location queries to target and turn the best into **publishable content**.
- The **quick-response inbox** drafts a **specific, on-point reply** to each enquiry that sounds like him — fast enough that he never loses a lead to slowness.
- A **lead-quality** signal helps him spend scarce time on real prospects, not tire-kickers.
- The funnel is fully usable **organically** — paid is optional, never required.

## Out of scope
- Real email/CRM send integration (observe the drafted reply + qualification, not live send).
- Paid campaign optimization (he has no budget).

## Discovery hints
Entry point(s): **/app/demo-leadgen**, then **Klíčová slova** + **Obsahový engine** (get found), **Lokální dominance**, and **Rychlá reakce** (respond). Do NOT script — Radek balances "get found" and "don't fumble a lead." A generic spam-reading reply, non-local keyword advice, or a volume-over-quality lead view is a finding.

## Grounding to check (L1)
Reply-draft prompt: does it receive the *actual enquiry text* + his service context, or reply generically? Keyword/content: locality + intent grounded? Lead-quality: real CRM-style signal or a placeholder score? Score grounding N/M per surface.
