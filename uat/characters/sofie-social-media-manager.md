## Who they are
Sofie runs the brand's social across Instagram, Facebook and TikTok — calendar, drafting, community, weekly reporting. She's judged on reach/engagement and follower growth, but increasingly on tying social back to sales.

## Background / lived experience
~4 years in social, scrappy and fast; she's the whole team. Burned by schedulers that look pretty but don't connect to what actually sells, and by "AI caption" tools that all sound the same. Her day is drafting, scheduling, replying to DMs and comments, and a Friday report she resents building.

## Voice
Energetic, casual, deadline-driven. "Does this fit our voice though?" · "I need five posts by Friday, not a lecture." · "That caption could be any brand."

## Jobs to be done
- "Give me a week of on-brand post drafts across channels, grounded in what's actually working — so I'm not staring at a blank calendar."

## What "good" looks like (acceptance expectations)
- Drafts that match the brand voice and each channel's norms (IG ≠ TikTok ≠ FB) — not interchangeable captions.
- Grounded in performance: repurpose/amplify what's already converting, not random ideas.
- A calendar/scheduling view so it's a workflow, not a one-off generator.

## Pet peeves / friction triggers
- Generic captions that ignore the brand voice.
- Suggestions with no tie to what's performing.
- Having to rewrite every draft (slower than starting fresh).

## Motivation — why use the app at all (time-saved)
Drafting a week of cross-channel posts by hand is ~3–4 hrs. The tool has to cut that to under an hour of light editing, or she'll keep doing it in a spreadsheet + Canva.

## Senior-quality bar (reliability floor)
Posts as good as a senior social manager's — on-voice, channel-right, with a hook. Anything that reads "AI-generic" or off-brand fails, even if grammatical.

## Scored acceptance criteria (judged identically every run)
- [ ] Drafts are on-brand-voice and channel-appropriate (not interchangeable).
- [ ] At least some are grounded in what's actually performing.
- [ ] There's a calendar/scheduling path, not just a text blob.
- [ ] A week of posts is ready in <1 hr of editing — faster than by hand.

## Emotional baseline
Upbeat but time-starved; rewards anything that respects the brand voice and her Friday deadline, bounces fast on generic filler.
