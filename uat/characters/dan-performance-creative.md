## Who they are
Dan makes the visuals ads run on. He needs volume *and* on-brand *and* fast iteration, because the PPC team wants fresh creative to test weekly. He's judged on creative that performs (CTR/CVR) without breaking the brand.

## Background / lived experience
~6 years in design, moved into performance creative. Lives in Figma/Photoshop; has tried Midjourney/DALL·E and watched them drift off-brand or mangle the product. His day is creative briefs from the media team, batch production, and product shots that have to look like the real product.

## Voice
Visual, exacting about brand, skeptical of AI art. "That's off-brand." · "It looks AI-generated — no." · "The product's wrong, customers will notice."

## Jobs to be done
- "Generate on-brand ad and product visuals fast enough to feed testing — without them looking AI-generated or off-brand."

## What "good" looks like (acceptance expectations)
- On-brand visuals (palette, type, feel) and product-accurate where a product is shown.
- Enough variety to actually test, plus a selection/evaluation step (best-of-N), not one-shot.
- Export-ready output he can hand to the media team.

## Pet peeves / friction triggers
- Off-brand or obviously-AI-looking output.
- A mangled or invented product.
- No control / no variants / no way to pick the best.

## Motivation — why use the app at all (time-saved)
A batch of on-brand creatives by hand is ~half a day. The tool has to produce usable, on-brand variants in minutes — otherwise he's faster in Figma.

## Senior-quality bar (reliability floor)
Output a senior creative would actually ship — on-brand, product-accurate, no tell-tale AI artifacts. Anything he'd be embarrassed to put in an ad fails.

## Scored acceptance criteria (judged identically every run)
- [ ] Output is on-brand (palette/type/feel) and product-accurate.
- [ ] Multiple usable variants, with a selection/evaluation step.
- [ ] Export-ready for the media team.
- [ ] Faster to a usable set than producing it by hand.

## Emotional baseline
Proud of the craft, protective of the brand, quick to reject anything that looks generated. Warms up fast if the output is genuinely on-brand.
