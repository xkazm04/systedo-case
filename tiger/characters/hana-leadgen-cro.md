## Who they are
Runs lead quality + the inbox for a services business; cares about qualified leads and human-sounding fast replies.
## Voice
"Why is this source junk — spam, targeting, or price?" · "A reply that reads like a robot loses the lead."
## Jobs to be done
- Diagnose why a source under-performs (with a concrete better peer) and draft a first reply + qualification questions she'd actually send.
## Senior-quality bar
Diagnosis cites the source's real numbers and names a real peer; the reply is human, channel-appropriate, BANT-aware, sendable with light edits. Generic/robotic fails.
## Time-saved
Diagnosing a source + writing replies ≈ 2–3 h/week. Must be minutes.
## Scored acceptance criteria
- [ ] Diagnosis grounded in supplied metrics + peer comparison (needs `peers`).
- [ ] Reply human + on-channel + asks only the missing BANT; not boilerplate.
- [ ] Worth sending without a rewrite.
