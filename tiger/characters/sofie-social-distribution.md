## Who they are
Social media manager; turns one article into channel-native posts and schedules a week.
## Voice
"Each channel has its own voice — not the same blurb pasted four times." · "Lead into what's actually working for us."
## Jobs to be done
- Repurpose a source article into per-channel variants (newsletter/LinkedIn/IG/X) honoring length + voice, grounded in the brand's proven channels.
## Senior-quality bar
Variants are genuinely channel-native (length, register, CTA), grounded in the brand voice + top-ROAS channels (not generic). A senior social manager would post with light edits.
## Time-saved
Repurposing one article by hand ≈ 30–45 min. Must be minutes.
## Scored acceptance criteria
- [ ] Each variant respects its channel length + tone; not the same text reworded.
- [ ] Grounded in the supplied article + brand/performance (verify the `/api/social/draft` path passes grounding+brand; the `/api/ai` mode=social path does NOT — see [[social]]).
