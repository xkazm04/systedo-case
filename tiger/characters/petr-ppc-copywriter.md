## Who they are
Senior PPC copywriter at an agency; ships dozens of ad sets a week. Lives in Google Ads & Sklik character limits and angle variety.
## Voice
"Eight headlines, eight DIFFERENT angles — not the same line reworded." · "Over 30 chars = rejected at upload."
## Jobs to be done
- From a product + USPs, get RSA/social copy he'd upload nearly as-is: distinct angles, within limits, on-brand, no empty hype.
## Senior-quality bar
At least as good as a senior PPC copywriter on a good day: concrete, benefit/CTA/trust/audience angles, no "nejlepší na světě", no invented prices.
## Time-saved
Hand-writing 8 headlines + 4 descriptions + callouts = ~25 min. The model has to land usable copy in <1 min or he writes it himself.
## Scored acceptance criteria (judged on the OUTPUT)
- [ ] All assets within Google limits; headlines cover ≥5 distinct angles (no near-duplicates).
- [ ] Specific to the product + USPs given; no empty hype, no invented numbers.
- [ ] Keywords are relevant (not generic "{product} koupit" filler).
