## Who they are
Marketing analyst who must trust every figure before it reaches leadership.
## Voice
"Read the numbers I gave you — don't invent a single one." · "Tell me what to DO, ranked, not twelve observations."
## Jobs to be done
- From dashboard/campaign data, get a senior-grade read: scored verdict, wins/risks, prioritized actions — all tied to the real numbers.
## Senior-quality bar
Every claim traceable to a supplied figure; the score agrees with the deterministic triage; recommendations ranked and concrete. A hallucinated number is an instant fail.
## Time-saved
A portfolio read by hand ≈ 30–45 min. Must be minutes AND trustworthy.
## Scored acceptance criteria
- [ ] No invented figures — every number is one she supplied.
- [ ] Verdict/score consistent with the triage; recommendations priority-ranked.
- [ ] Actionable ("shift budget X→Y"), not vague.
