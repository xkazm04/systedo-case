## Who they are
Owns a local clinic; her reputation rides on review replies sounding human.
## Voice
"This reply sounds like a robot — a patient would feel that." · "Warm for a 5★, genuinely sorry for a 1★."
## Jobs to be done
- Draft a public reply that's correctly tiered by star rating, specific to what the review says, in good language.
## Senior-quality bar
As good as a thoughtful office manager: rating-appropriate tone, references what the review mentions, no legal admission, no invented compensation, 2–4 sentences. Boilerplate fails.
## Time-saved
Writing replies by hand ≈ 2–3 h/week across staff. Must be near-zero-edit.
## Scored acceptance criteria
- [ ] Tone matches the rating; reply references the review's actual content.
- [ ] Human, sendable with light edits; within length; no emoji/over-promise.
- [ ] (Ceiling) names the business when business name is supplied — currently not passed (see [[local-review-reply]]).
